Food Ordering App

A full-stack application to manage users and food orders. This application enables various restaurants to register and manage their staff, including roles like Branch Manager, Kitchen Manager, and Cashier. A Super Admin oversees the platform, registering restaurants and assigning restaurant admins. Each restaurant admin is responsible for defining roles and adding staff members. The Kitchen Manager manages the menu, including adding items, creating new toppings, and updating order statuses. Customers can browse menus, place orders, and track the status of their orders through the application. This application is deployed on Vercel for front-end, Railway for back-end and Cloudinary fro media storage.
